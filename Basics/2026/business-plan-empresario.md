## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**The Business:**
AI HUEVOS builds agent infrastructure for LatAm enterprises. We enter through high-value workflow automation projects, then productize into Director AI—the platform where enterprises, agents, and capital transact with full traceability.

**The Model:**
- **Phase 1 (Months 0-12):** Workflows prove ROI, fund infrastructure build (30-40% reinvestment)
- **Phase 2 (Months 12-24):** Infrastructure productized, recurring revenue scales
- **Phase 3 (Months 24-36):** Platform economics emerge, vertical replication creates moat

**The Assets We're Building:**
1. **Director AI Platform** — Reusable infrastructure (85% margin)
2. **Industry Playbooks** — Vertical replication (Opera PMS → hotels, F&B systems → restaurants)
3. **Proprietary Benchmark Data** — Cross-company insights
4. **Enterprise Relationships** — Deep trust with family offices and complex enterprises

**The Ask:**
$100K-150K strategic capital (debt or revenue-based financing preferred) to:
- Unlock full capacity (co-founder full-time = 6 clients)
- Productize Director AI (infrastructure asset)
- Build industry playbooks (reusable assets)

**The Return:**
- Profitability by Month 12-18
- $540K revenue at full capacity (67% GM)
- Self-sustaining operations by Month 18
- Long-term asset value (Director AI, playbooks, relationships)
