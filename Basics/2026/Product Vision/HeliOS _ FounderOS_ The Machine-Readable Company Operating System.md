#### II. THE HELIOS OPERATING SYSTEM: EXECUTIVE FAQ

##### Strategic Context: Defending the Mechanics

While the Press Release visualizes the future, this FAQ serves as the operational defense of the HeliOS mechanics. For high-performers, "process" often feels like "bureaucracy." This document proves the "So What?" by demonstrating how machine-readable systems—costing only \~1% of payroll to maintain—actually increase freedom by reducing cognitive load and clarifying ownership. It is the bridge between Mochary’s theory and version-controlled execution.

###### *1\. How does HeliOS differ from a traditional company Wiki or Notion?*

Traditional tools are "passive wikis"—static repositories where info goes to die. HeliOS is an  **Active OS** . Because the core data (AORs, OKRs, KPIs) is stored in YAML/CSV on GitHub, the system uses  **GitHub Actions for operational compliance** . It doesn't just store info; it validates it. If an AOR is missing a backup DRI or a KPI exceeds a Red threshold, the system alerts the owner automatically.

###### *2\. What is the "Mochary Plus" distinction?*

HeliOS is the operationalization of  *The Great CEO Within* . While the book provides theory, HeliOS provides the  **version-controlled specs** . We transform human-language advice into machine-readable schemas. This allows for AI-automated reporting and "Process Freshness" monitoring, moving the burden of system maintenance from the founders to the AI layer.

###### *4\. What are the specific roles of the AI agents?*

* **NotebookLM (The Librarian/Coach):**  The  **Conversational Interface**  for discovery and onboarding. Ask it: "Where does the payroll process live?" or "What is our 1:1 template?" It queries the AOR "Routing Table" to resolve ownership questions instantly.  
* **Claude Code (The Logic Engine/Analyst):**  The  **Automator** . It validates YAML schemas and generates automated status reports every Monday at 9:00 AM. It performs SPOF audits and transforms raw meeting notes into structured "Next Actions" in the GTD system.

###### *5\. How does HeliOS prevent bureaucracy and manage costs?*

The system adheres to the  **"Span of Control"**  rule (4–8 reports per manager). Maintenance is lean, requiring only  **\~1% of payroll**  (CEO: 2 hrs/week; Managers: 1 hr/week). By treating AORs as the "Company's Routing Table," we resolve the "tragedy of the commons" without adding layers of middle management.

###### *6\. What does the "First 24 Hours" of implementation look like?*

* **Hour 1:**  Setup the GitHub repository and folder structure.  
* **Hour 4:**  Instantiate YAML templates (AORs, OKRs, KPIs) and log the first  **Energy Audit** .  
* **Hour 24:**  Train NotebookLM on the repository to enable the first conversational queries.

###### *7\. What is the "AI Assistant Contract" regarding security?*

Data protection is managed through a three-tier approval workflow:

* **Tier 1 (Internal Templates):**  AI-generated, Manager-reviewed (e.g., JDs, blog outlines).  
* **Tier 2 (External Comms):**  Multi-level review required before sending (e.g., Investor updates, offers).  
* **Tier 3 (Strategic):**  Human-led, AI-assisted (e.g., Term sheets, hiring/firing).  **Prohibited Use:**  AI may never make hiring/firing decisions or access live cap-table data.

###### *8\. How do we quantify success?*

We track 5 critical "Success Metrics":

1. **Decision Speed:**  Time from Issue identified to RAPID decision.  
2. **Agreement Completion Rate:**  Target 85–90%+.  
3. **Meeting Hours Reduced:**  Transitioning from verbal updates to written-first.  
4. **SPOF Count Reduction:**  Number of functions with a 100% trained backup.  
5. **Time in Zone of Genius:**  Reclaiming 20+ hours for high-leverage work.
