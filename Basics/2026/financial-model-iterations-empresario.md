# FINANCIAL MODEL ITERATIONS - EMPRESARIO MINDSET
## What to Change for Corporation Building vs. Startup Pitch

---

## KEY DIFFERENCES: EMPRENDEDOR vs. EMPRESARIO

| Aspect | Emprendedor (Startup) | Empresario (Corporation) |
|--------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| **Focus** | Growth, scalability | Profitability, sustainability |
| **Capital** | Venture capital, equity | Revenue-based financing, debt |
| **Exit** | Build to sell | Build to own |
| **Metrics** | ARR, burn rate, CAC | Cash flow, net profit, assets |
| **Timeline** | 5-7 year exit | Long-term ownership |
| **Reinvestment** | Growth at all costs | Asset building (20-40%) |
| **Risk** | High growth, high risk | Profitable, sustainable |

---

## FINANCIAL MODEL ITERATIONS NEEDED

### 1. Reframe Capital Ask

**Current (Startup):**
- $150K-200K at $1.6M valuation (9-12% equity)
- Venture capital structure
- Exit-focused narrative

**New (Corporation):**
- $100K-150K revenue-based financing (2-3x return cap)
- OR convertible debt (20% discount, $2M cap)
- OR strategic partnership
- No equity dilution (preferred)

**Why:**
- Empresario mindset = preserve equity, build to own
- Revenue-based financing aligns with cash flow
- Strategic partnership adds value beyond capital
