# AI HUEVOS - Business Information Analysis

## 1. MISSION & VISION

### Core Purpose
AI HUEVOS positions itself as a **creative technology consultancy** that bridges human creativity with AI automation. The core philosophy: **"Transformar requiere huevos"** (Transformation requires courage).

### Mission
To help B2B companies (particularly SMEs and mid-market) systematize and automate their operations through custom AI workflows and intelligent agents, while maintaining the human touch in strategic decision-making.

### Long-term Vision
- Transform from services to product: Building scalable SaaS offerings based on proven consulting methodologies
- Geographic expansion: Currently Colombia/LATAM, expanding to US market
- Revenue target: $500K-$1M ARR within 18-24 months through diagnostic tool productization
