## Core Values, Beliefs & Decision-Making Patterns

**Generated from**: 12 pure founding team meetings (Daniel Cardona, Daniel Restrepo, Tomas Shuk)

## Executive Summary

AI Huevos' founding team demonstrates a distinctive DNA characterized by **bootstrap sustainability**, **validation-before-scale**, and **showcase-driven commercial strategy**. Unlike typical VC-backed startups, the founders prioritize financial independence, explicitly targeting established enterprises over startups, and building with small, technically-leveraged teams using agentic AI development.

The co-founders exhibit complementary archetypes: **Daniel Cardona** as the systems architect focused on sustainable business models and technical infrastructure, **Daniel Restrepo** as the commercial strategist who ensures everything validates in real markets and demonstrates tangible value, and **Tomas Shuk** as the ecosystem builder thinking at portfolio and strategic partnership levels.

Their operational philosophy centers on **discovery-driven development** (conducting 40+ qualitative interviews before building), **starting simple then sophisticating** based on real feedback, and maintaining **rigorous documentation and measurable outcomes**. They explicitly reject aggressive VC roadshows, startup customers, premature scaling, and complexity-first approaches.
