## What Was Eliminated

### Non-Essential Removed:
- ❌ Meeting IDs (unless critical for traceability)
- ❌ Verbose quotes (kept summaries)
- ❌ Redundant examples (kept best 1-2)
- ❌ Placeholder text ("To be extracted", "Additional meetings")
- ❌ Duplicate information across sections
- ❌ Verbose "Why it works" explanations
- ❌ Historical context that doesn't inform decisions
- ❌ Repetitive "Implication" statements
- ❌ Over-detailed meeting evidence (kept key principles)
- ❌ Redundant cross-references

### Essential Kept:
- ✅ Decision patterns and frameworks
- ✅ Quantifiable thresholds ($26K/year, $1.5M+ potential)
- ✅ Win/loss patterns
- ✅ Customer qualification criteria
- ✅ Key learnings (Vistage failure patterns)
- ✅ Partner collaboration principles
- ✅ Strategic recommendations
- ✅ Core values and principles
- ✅ Founder archetypes and communication patterns
- ✅ All actionable guidelines

## Context Engineering Optimizations

### For AI Reasoning:

1. **Pattern-First Structure**
   - Patterns stated before examples
   - Enables pattern matching and generalization
   - Decision tables where applicable

2. **Explicit Decision Criteria**
   - Quantifiable thresholds highlighted ($26K/year, $1.5M+)
   - Clear if/then logic
   - Decision matrices for quick reference

3. **Hierarchical Information**
   - Most important insights at top
   - Supporting details below
   - Reduced cognitive load

4. **Structured for Retrieval**
   - Clear headings enable semantic search
   - Key terms highlighted
   - Relationships explicit
   - Tables for comparison matrices

5. **Consolidated Cross-References**
   - Simplified source references
   - Removed verbose metadata
   - Clear file relationships
