## Story Arc: Problem → Solution → Transformation

### ACT 1: The Problem (Diagnostic)

**Setting**: Large enterprises in Latin America struggling with operational inefficiencies

**The Challenge**:
- **142 systems** creating **277 fricciones** (frictions) - Bolivian Foods example
- Decision bottlenecks limiting operational capacity (Samuel at Bolivian Foods)
- Manual processes consuming valuable time (Comversa, Hotel LTH)
- Fragmented data preventing holistic visibility
- Integration challenges between systems (Hotel LTH: maintenance + procurement)

**The Impact**:
- Lost productivity: Teams spending days on tasks that should take minutes
- Bottleneck delays: Single decision-makers blocking progress
- Missed opportunities: Data exists but isn't actionable
- Growing frustration: Technology promised efficiency but delivered complexity

**The Question**: "How do we fix this without disrupting what's working?"

---

### ACT 2: The Solution (Intelligent Automation)

**The Approach**: Diagnostic-first, then systematic automation

**What We Do**:
1. **Diagnose** the inefficiencies (identify $1.5M+ in value potential)
2. **Automate** specific processes (back office, contracts, service desk)
3. **Integrate** systems to create single source of truth
4. **Improve** continuously through feedback loops

**How It Works**:
- Start with **one process** (e.g., invoice reconciliation)
- Automate it in **2-3 weeks** (quick win)
- Demonstrate **measurable results** (2 days → 15 minutes)
- Expand systematically to **more processes**
- Build toward **autonomous agent workflows**

**The Key**: Technology that learns and improves, not just executes

**Partner Learning (GLT)**: Data enrichment BEFORE automation is critical - enrich internal data to personalize follow-up before automating processes.

---

### ACT 3: The Transformation (Operating System)

**The Vision**: Your business becomes more autonomous

**Short-term (3-6 months)**:
- Specific processes automated
- Time savings realized
- Manual work reduced
- Visibility increased

**Medium-term (6-12 months)**:
- Multiple processes integrated
- Systems talking to each other
- Decision bottlenecks reduced
- Data-driven decisions enabled

**Long-term (1-2 years)**:
- Operating system that orchestrates operations
- AI agents handling routine tasks
- Employees supervising and improving workflows
- Business scales without proportional cost increase

**The Result**: You focus on strategy while the system handles operations

## Partner Learnings Integration

### GLT AI Implementation Learnings (Peer Learning)

**Data Enrichment Before Automation**:
- Enrich internal data BEFORE automating to personalize follow-up
- **Application**: Diagnostic phase must include data quality assessment

**Maximum 3 Concurrent Initiatives**:
- Limiting to 3 initiatives ensures concrete results instead of dispersion
- **Application**: Apply "no más de 3 proyectos" principle to all engagements

**Account Growth vs. New Acquisition**:
- Focus on growing existing accounts in addition to acquiring new ones
- **Application**: Expansion strategy should include account growth, not just new customers

**System Integration for Adoption**:
- CRM + Power BI integration enables sales team to access categorized information
- **Application**: Integration must enable end-user access, not just backend automation
