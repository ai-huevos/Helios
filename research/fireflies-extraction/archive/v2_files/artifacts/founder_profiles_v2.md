## Daniel Cardona: The Architect

**Role:** Systems thinker who translates vision into architecture and sustainable business models

### Background Context
- Technology and product leadership
- Growth and marketing expertise
- Roadmap and system architecture focus
- Financial realist focused on sustainable business models

### Communication Style
- **Technical but accessible**: Translates complexity into clarity
- **System-thinking**: "Sistema operativo" (Operating system) vision
- **Execution-focused**: Gets things built and delivered
- **Integration-minded**: Connects different systems and platforms
- **Financial realist**: Focuses on sustainable business models, bootstrap mentality

### Key Phrases & Language Fingerprints
- "Roadmap de automatización" (Automation roadmap)
- "Sistema operativo tecnológico" (Technological operating system)
- "Mejorar la eficiencia mediante inteligencia artificial" (Improve efficiency through AI)
- "El cliente ideal no son startups, sino empresas con alto willingness to pay" (Ideal customer is not startups, but enterprises with high willingness to pay)
- "Modelo de negocio sostenible, evitando inversiones externas" (Sustainable business model, avoiding external investments)
- "Equipo pequeño y técnico, con un enfoque en entregas rápidas" (Small technical team, focused on fast deliveries)

### Decision Patterns
- **Technology as foundation**: Build systems that enable
- **Prioritize 3 projects max**: "No más de tres proyectos a la vez"
- **Show, don't tell**: Demonstrates with working prototypes
- **Integration over isolation**: Connects systems for better outcomes
- **Bootstrap sustainability**: Financial independence over growth-at-all-costs
- **Customer profile precision**: Enterprise only, complex ops, high willingness to pay

### Characteristics
1. **Technical Infrastructure Designer**: Builds scalable systems
2. **Financial Realist**: Bootstrap mentality, sustainable business models
3. **Customer Profile Precision**: Explicitly rejects startups, targets enterprises
4. **Detail-Oriented Executor**: Gets things built and delivered

### Strengths
- Technical architecture and design
- Product development and delivery
- Marketing and growth systems
- Ability to build platforms that scale
- Financial sustainability focus
- Customer qualification precision

## Daniel Restrepo: The Validator

**Role:** Commercial strategist who ensures everything validates in real markets and demonstrates tangible value

### Background Context
- Operations-focused approach
- Emphasis on validation and real-world testing
- Strong focus on customer outcomes
- Commercial strategist ensuring market validation

### Communication Style
- **Practical and execution-focused**: "Validar en entornos reales"
- **Customer-centric**: "Enfoque en el cliente y los resultados"
- **Data-driven**: References specific metrics and outcomes
- **Process-oriented**: Thinks in workflows and systems
- **Validation-driven**: "¿Cómo validamos esto?" (How do we validate this?)

### Key Phrases & Language Fingerprints
- "Validar propuestas en entornos reales antes de escalar" (Validate in real environments before scaling)
- "Enfoque en el cliente" (Focus on customer)
- "Feedback loop" (Repeatedly references improvement cycles)
- "Automatizaciones" (Automations) - Operational focus
- "Construir showcases rápidos basados en experiencias previas" (Build quick showcases based on previous experiences)
- "Mostrar casos de éxito concretos" (Show concrete success cases)

### Decision Patterns
- **Test first, scale later**: Validation before expansion
- **Customer-first decisions**: What benefits the client
- **Avoid fundraising distractions**: "Fundraising es una gran distracción"
- **Build from the bottom up**: Start with specific processes, build to systems
- **Showcase-driven**: Build showcases quickly based on previous experiences

### Characteristics
1. **Real-World Validation Champion**: Ensures everything validates in real environments
2. **Showcase Builder**: Builds tangible demonstrations of value
3. **Simplicity Advocate**: Start simple, sophisticate later
4. **Strategic Fundraiser**: When fundraising, ensures it's strategic, not desperate

### Strengths
- Operational execution
- Customer empathy and understanding
- Process design and automation thinking
- Ability to translate vision into actionable steps
- Validation-first mindset
- Showcase-driven commercial strategy
