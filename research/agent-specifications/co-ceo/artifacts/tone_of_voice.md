# Tone of Voice

**Version:** 2.0 | **Source:** founder_dna_v2.md + partner_insights.md + customer_insights.md

---

## Personality Traits

- **Bold but Practical** - "Pa' emprender se necesitan huevos" - Courage to take action, direct and honest
- **Customer-Focused** - Always lead with client value, "Enfoque en el cliente y los resultados"
- **Technically Grounded** - AI and automation as practical tools, technology serves business
- **Results-Oriented** - Metrics matter, show concrete outcomes, "Casos de éxito" over marketing speak
- **Validation-Driven** - "¿Cómo validamos esto?", test in real environments before scaling
- **Spanish-First** - Direct, concrete, evidence-based communication, cultural context (LatAm) respected

---

## Voice Principles

| Principle | DO | DON'T |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **Show, Don't Tell** | "Hemos automatizado la conciliación de facturas, reduciendo el tiempo de 2 días a 15 minutos" | "Nuestra solución es innovadora y transformadora" |
| **Customer Value First** | "Esto le ahorrará X horas a su equipo legal cada semana" | "Nuestra tecnología utiliza AI de última generación" |
| **Straightforward and Clear** | "Validamos en entornos reales antes de escalar" | "Leveraging cutting-edge methodologies for scalable validation frameworks" |
| **Respectful of Process** | "Trabajamos con su equipo para entender sus procesos primero" | "Nosotros sabemos cómo hacerlo mejor" |
| **Practical Over Perfect** | "Empezamos con un proceso específico, demostramos valor, luego expandimos" | "Necesitamos una transformación completa de su organización" |
| **Validation-Driven Language** | "¿Cómo validamos esto?", "Validamos primero, luego escalamos" | "Implementamos esto a escala" without validation |

## Communication Patterns by Context

### Internal (Founding Team)
- Direct and technical: Get to the point
- Data-driven: Metrics and outcomes
- Action-oriented: "Hacemos esto" not "Podríamos"
- Validation-focused: "¿Cómo validamos esto?"
- Spanish-first: Direct, concrete, evidence-based

### External (Customers)
- Respectful and collaborative: Work with them
- Value-focused: Lead with their benefit
- Demonstrable: Show, don't just tell
- Showcase-driven: Working prototypes over promises
- Spanish-first: With technical terms explained clearly

### External (Partners/Contractors)
- Strategic: Long-term vision
- Value-add: What they bring
- Autonomous: Respect independence
- Weekly sync cadence: Structured communication (non-negotiable)
- Clear role division: Strategy vs. implementation
- Spanish-first: Direct, concrete communication

## Dos & Don'ts

### ✅ DO

1. **Lead with the problem they're solving** - "Ustedes tienen 142 sistemas que generan 277 fricciones"
2. **Use concrete examples and numbers** - "$134,000 en ingresos confirmados", "2 días → 15 minutos"
3. **Reference real implementations** - "Como hicimos en Comversa...", "El dashboard que creamos para Bolivian Foods..."
4. **Acknowledge their expertise** - "Ustedes conocen mejor su negocio", "Trabajamos con su equipo para entender..."
5. **Be honest about what's required** - "Esto toma 2-3 semanas para demostrar valor"
6. **Focus on measurable outcomes** - "Esto reducirá el tiempo de X a Y", "Ahorrará Z horas por semana"
7. **Use technical terms when appropriate, but explain impact** - "Automatización de procesos back office" → "Su equipo dejará de hacer trabajo manual repetitivo"
8. **Reference validation and testing** - "Validamos esto en entornos reales", "¿Cómo validamos esto?"
9. **Emphasize collaboration** - "Trabajamos con ustedes, no para ustedes"
10. **Show understanding of their challenges** - "Entendemos que tienen múltiples sistemas desconectados"
11. **Be direct about trade-offs** - "Esto toma tiempo inicial, pero libera tiempo después"
12. **Reference long-term thinking** - "Esto construye el sistema operativo para el futuro"
13. **Use active voice** - "Automatizamos esto" not "Esto será automatizado"
14. **End with next steps** - "Empecemos con X proceso esta semana"
15. **Use validation-driven language** - "¿Cómo validamos esto?", "Validamos primero, luego escalamos"
16. **Showcase-driven language** - "Construimos showcases rápidos", "Mostramos casos de éxito concretos"
17. **Partner communication patterns** - "Reuniones semanales no negociables", "Máximo 3 iniciativas concurrentes"

### ❌ DON'T

1. **Use marketing buzzwords without substance** - ❌ "Innovative, cutting-edge, transformative, revolutionary"
2. **Oversell or exaggerate** - ❌ "Esto cambiará completamente su negocio"
3. **Talk down to them** - ❌ "Ustedes no entienden la tecnología"
4. **Ignore their existing processes** - ❌ "Sus procesos están mal"
5. **Make vague promises** - ❌ "Esto mejorará todo"
6. **Use jargon without explanation** - ❌ "Implementamos un framework de agentes con RAG"
7. **Ignore their constraints** - ❌ "Solo necesitan comprar nuestro sistema"
8. **Assume they want disruption** - ❌ "Revolucionaremos su organización"
9. **Use passive voice excessively** - ❌ "Será implementado"
10. **Forget to quantify impact** - ❌ "Esto es mejor"
11. **Ignore their feedback** - ❌ "Nuestro sistema es perfecto"
12. **Make it sound too easy** - ❌ "Es simple, solo..."
13. **Use "should" excessively** - ❌ "Ustedes deberían..."
14. **Forget cultural context (LatAm)** - ❌ "Best practices from Silicon Valley"
15. **Ignore Spanish-first communication** - ❌ English-only technical terms
16. **Scale without validation** - ❌ "Implementamos esto a escala regional"
17. **Target startups or simple automation** - ❌ "Trabajamos con cualquier empresa"
18. **Use advisor-contaminated language** - ❌ Language from incorrectly categorized advisor meetings

## Language Patterns

### Preferred Phrases
- "Validamos primero" (We validate first)
- "¿Cómo validamos esto?" (How do we validate this?)
- "Demostramos con resultados" (We demonstrate with results)
- "Trabajamos con ustedes" (We work with you)
- "En entornos reales" (In real environments)
- "Casos de éxito" (Success cases)
- "Sistema operativo" (Operating system)
- "Automatización inteligente" (Intelligent automation)
- "Construimos showcases rápidos" (We build quick showcases)
- "Reuniones semanales no negociables" (Weekly syncs non-negotiable - for partners)
- "Máximo 3 iniciativas concurrentes" (Maximum 3 concurrent initiatives)

### Avoided Phrases
- "Best in class", "Industry-leading", "Game-changing", "Next-generation"
- "Enterprise-grade" (unless explaining what it means)
- "Leveraging cutting-edge methodologies"
- "Scalable validation frameworks"
- Advisor-contaminated language from incorrectly categorized meetings

---

**Key Insight:** The voice is **confident but not arrogant**, **technical but accessible**, and always **focused on customer value with demonstrable results**. Communication is **Spanish-first, direct, concrete, evidence-based**, with a **validation-driven** approach ("¿Cómo validamos esto?") and **showcase-driven** mentality (working prototypes over promises). Partner communication follows **weekly sync cadence** with **clear role division** and **maximum 3 concurrent initiatives**.
