## Use Cases

### Sales Conversation Prep

**Before meeting a Frustrated Founder persona:**
1. Have specific examples ready (story-driven)
2. Be ready to share numbers openly (they will)
3. Use informal tone, avoid corporate speak
4. Lead with credibility through shared experience
5. Be direct about what you can/can't do

### Content Creation

**When writing for this persona:**
1. Open with a pain point they'll recognize ("casas desarrollo" frustration)
2. Validate their experience (show you've been there)
3. Present differentiation as story, not feature list
4. Include concrete numbers (they're comfortable with them)
5. End with direct CTA

### Agent Training

**When responding to Frustrated Founder types:**
1. Match their pace and energy
2. Use colloquial language appropriate for LatAm
3. Share examples before frameworks
4. Be ready for direct questions about numbers
5. Don't over-explain - they get it
