## PART 4: OBJECTION PATTERNS

### Value Not Understood

| Objection Pattern | Their Words | Our Response Approach |
|-------------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Customer doesn't get methodology | "No entiende la propuesta valor nuestra" | Lead with education, use examples |
| Customer sees same as competitors | "No entiende el riesgo que estamos disminuyendo" | Frame as risk reduction, not just cost |
| Customer wants same as before | "Le parece normal" | Highlight differentiation with proof |

**Validated Response:**
> "Let me show you what that looks like in practice. [Example] This saved [Company] [specific result]. That's the difference."

---

### Maturity Mismatch

| Objection Pattern | Signal | Our Response Approach |
|-------------------|--------|----------------------|
| No prior tech projects | Asking basic questions | Pre-qualify, possibly decline |
| Don't understand timelines | "¿Por qué tanta demora?" | Education before engagement |
| Want magic solutions | "¿No puede ser más rápido?" | Set realistic expectations |

**Validated Response:**
> "We work best with companies who've done this before. If this is your first technology project, we might not be the right fit—yet."

---

### Price Concerns

| Objection Pattern | Signal | Our Response Approach |
|-------------------|--------|----------------------|
| Comparing to cheaper options | "Es más caro que..." | Show total cost of ownership |
| Not seeing ROI | "¿Cómo sé que vale?" | Diagnostic first, prove value |
| Budget constraints | "No tenemos presupuesto" | Phase approach, quick wins first |

**Validated Response:**
> "You're right—we're not the cheapest. But think about what you've spent on projects that didn't deliver. Our clients tell us we're the cheapest mistake they never made."
