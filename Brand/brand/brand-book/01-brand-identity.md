### English

- Companies with **500+ employees**

- Companies running on WhatsApp, Excel, and people's heads

- High willingness to pay for enterprise solutions

**Common Pain Points:**
- Disconnected systems (142 systems creating 277 frictions - Bolivian Foods example)

- Fragmented data preventing holistic visibility
- Integration challenges between systems
- Trapped knowledge, no visibility

- **Bolivian Foods:** Revenue-generating unit with 142 systems, 277 fricciones, decision bottlenecks

- **Los Tajibos:** Service desk operations optimization

### Secondary: Operations & Technology Leaders

- COOs seeking operational visibility
- CTOs managing legacy systems
- Founders of traditional businesses needing digital transformation
- Decision makers who've spent millions on SAP/Oracle but see minimal adoption

**We are NOT for everyone** — we target enterprises with complex operations and high willingness to pay, not startups or simple automation needs.

**Red Flags (Not ICP):**
- ❌ Strategy-focused without execution capability
- ❌ Limited follow-up infrastructure
- ❌ Financial control issues
- ❌ Not enterprise-scale operations
- ❌ Low willingness to pay
