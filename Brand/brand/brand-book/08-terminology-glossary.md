## Language Rules Summary

### ✅ Acceptable in Spanish Sections

- Technical terms: **showcase**, **showcases**, **AI**, **API**, **CRM**
- Product names: **Director AI**, **Macroprocesos**
- Business terms: **family office**, **enterprise**, **leverage**, **bootstrap**
- Code/technical: File paths, hex codes, CSS, HTML

### ❌ Not Acceptable

- Mixed-language sentences: "La paleta balancea profesionalismo" → Full Spanish, then full English
- English phrases in Spanish content (unless technical term)
- Spanish phrases in English content (unless proper noun)

---

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Technical terms**: Use English term in both languages when it's industry-standard (AI, API, CRM)
2. **Brand terms**: Use consistent translation from this glossary
3. **Product names**: Use as-is (Director AI, Macroprocesos)
4. **Code examples**: Always in English
5. **File paths**: Always in English
6. **Hex codes/RGB**: Language-neutral
