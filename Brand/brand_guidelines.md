# AI Huevos - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Brand Name**: AI Huevos
**Designer**: Nocturna Studio
**Language**: Spanish (primary)

## Brand DNA

### Differentiators
- **Creatividad como núcleo**: Transformar procesos aburridos o rígidos en experiencias creativas
- **Facilidad y simplicidad**: Soluciones claras, directas y sin rodeos
- **Modelo de trabajo flexible**: Contratos de 1 año, con fee mensual + horas de mantenimiento
- **Uso inteligente de la IA**: Como herramienta de apoyo, no como protagonista

### Tone of Voice
- **Claro**: Comunica de forma transparente, sin rodeos ni ambigüedades
- **Sencillo**: Reduce lo complejo a lo esencial, haciendo que cada interacción sea intuitiva
- **Directo**: Va al punto con precisión, sin adornos innecesarios
- **Humano**: Habla con empatía y cercanía, creando conexiones genuinas

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (use in similar proportions)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Background, text on dark
- **Black**: `#000000` - Text, logo negative
- **Yellow**: `#FFE03D` - Primary accent, egg character
- **Blue**: `#4D9FFF` - Primary accent, egg character

### Secondary Colors
- **Purple**: `#B8A0FF` - Details, accents (use sparingly)
- **Red/Orange**: `#FF5C3D` - CTAs, buttons (minimal usage)

### Web-Specific Colors
- **Light Gray**: `#E5E5E5` - Backgrounds, dividers
- **Dark Gray**: `#9B9B9B` - Secondary text, highlights

### Color Combinations (approved)
- White on Black, Yellow, Blue, Purple, Red
- Black on White, Yellow
- Blue on White, Yellow, Purple, Red
- Yellow on Black, Blue, Purple
- Purple on Black, Yellow, Blue
- Red on Black, Yellow

### Color Usage Rules
- **Diseño**: Texts always black or white (prioritize contrast). Color only for accents.
- **Fotografía**: Texts primarily white and black. Other colors sparingly for special pieces.

## Typography

### Primary Font: Scandia
- **Weights**: Regular, Medium
- **Usage**:
  - Medium: Headlines, short texts
  - Regular: Body copy, running text
- **Case**: Always sentence case
- **Files**: `Brand/fonts/Scandia/`

### Secondary Font: Geist Mono
- **Weight**: Regular
- **Usage**: CTAs, short highlights, code-like text
- **Case**: Always lowercase
- **Files**: `Brand/fonts/Geist_Mono/`

### Type Hierarchy
- **Display Headline**: 130px, -1% tracking
- **H1 Headline**: 100px, -1% tracking
- **H2 Headline**: 75px, -1% tracking
- **H3 Headline**: 60px, -1% tracking
- **H4 Headline**: 36px, -1% tracking
- **Eyebrow**: 36px, -1% tracking
- **Paragraphs**: 24px, -1% tracking
- **CTAs**: 20px, 0% tracking (Geist Mono)

## Characters (Personajes)

The egg-shaped characters are the foundation of the graphic system.

### Character Versions
1. **Sin rostro** (no face): Ornamental use, text accompaniment
2. **Con rostro** (with face): Express ideas and emotions
3. **Con cuerpo** (with body): Stronger emotions, interaction with space and people

### Character Colors & Values
- **Claridad** (Yellow): Clear, calm expression
- **Ingenio** (Purple): Clever, playful pose
- **Curiosidad** (Blue): Inquisitive, friendly smile
- **Creatividad** (Yellow): Thoughtful, inspired look
- **Optimismo** (Orange): Joyful, energetic arms up

### Character Usage
- Can be used on colored backgrounds (not just egg shape)
- Can be abstracted (egg shapes without faces)
- Can accompany text as details
- Can interact with photography
- Can take different forms (speech bubbles, cartons, etc.)

## Photography Guidelines

### Style
- Natural light, warm tones, modern environments
- Authentic, collaborative, close atmosphere
- People working together, conversing, or focused individually
- Dynamic energy, good vibes

### Technical Requirements
- Clean compositions
- Focus on human interaction
- Natural expressions (avoid rigid poses)
- Modern, uncluttered locations

### Casting
- Men and women 28-40 years old
- Diverse physical characteristics, skin tones, hair colors
- Relaxed wardrobe in neutral tones

### Locations
- Work spaces, offices, home offices, cafés
- Neutral color dominance
- Uncluttered, minimal distractions

### Photo Types
1. **Main Photography**: Full scenes with people
2. **Close-up Photography**: Detail shots of actions (typing, phone use, etc.)
3. **Color Background Photography**: Portraits with brand color backgrounds (for testimonials, quotes)

## Logo Usage

### Logo Versions
- **Primary**: Black text "ai huevos" with yellow egg character as "o"
- **Blue version**: White text with blue egg character
- **Applications**: Works on black, yellow, blue, purple, orange backgrounds

### Logo Protection
- **Clear space**: Height of egg character (X) on all sides
- **Minimum size**:
  - Digital: 170px width
  - Print: 2.1cm width

## Animation Principles

### Character Animation
- Simple, easy movements
- Characters can appear or move simply
- Avoid complex animations

### Piece Animation
- Always play with characters, forms, and movements
- Simple text animations to accompany
- Keep it dynamic and fun

## Content Themes

### Key Messages
- "Pa' emprender se necesitan huevos" (You need eggs/courage to start a business)
- "AI tastes better with eggs"
- "No sé si automatizar o freír" (Don't know whether to automate or fry)
- "Hay huevos y hay AI huevos" (There are eggs and there are AI eggs)

### Content Pillars
- Creativity as core
- Simplicity and clarity
- Intelligent AI use
- Flexible work model
- Human-centered approach

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use sentence case for Scandia
- Use lowercase for Geist Mono
- Prioritize contrast in color combinations
- Keep character animations simple
- Use characters consistently with brand values
- Maintain playful, human tone

### Don'ts
- Don't use all caps unless specifically required
- Don't use colors not in the palette
- Don't make characters too complex
- Don't use imagery that feels corporate or stiff
- Don't skip the logo clear space
- Don't use logo smaller than minimum size
