## Audience Calibration

| Audience | Tone | Detail Level | Format |
|----------|------|--------------|--------|
| Investors | Formal, metrics-driven | High-level + key numbers | Structured update |
| Clients | Professional, collaborative | Task-specific | Email or doc |
| Partners | Casual professional | Strategic | Email |
| Team | Direct, casual | Detailed | Slack/quick doc |

## Quality Checklist

Before sending:

- [ ] Subject line is clear and action-oriented
- [ ] TL;DR in first paragraph
- [ ] One clear call to action
- [ ] Proofread for Spanish grammar
- [ ] Appropriate for audience (tone, detail)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (short paragraphs)
- [ ] No orphan context (reader has what they need)
